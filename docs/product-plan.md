# Product Plan

## Roles

### Tenant

- Search properties by city, locality, budget, bedrooms, furnishing, parking, and pet policy.
- View property details with photos, video, amenities, map context, and landlord verification status.
- Save favorites, contact landlords, and schedule visits.

### Landlord

- Register and verify profile.
- Upload properties, images, videos, and documents.
- Manage inquiries, scheduled visits, leads, analytics, and subscription plans.

### Admin

- Verify landlords.
- Moderate content and media.
- Manage users, listings, subscriptions, payments, and reports.
- Track marketplace health and revenue.

## MVP Build Order

1. Auth, roles, and landlord verification states.
2. Property listing CRUD and public property search.
3. Direct S3 image uploads and gallery.
4. Tenant inquiries and visit scheduling.
5. Admin moderation queue.
6. Payments, subscription plans, and premium listings.
7. Video upload processing and real-time messaging.

## Revenue

- Listing fee: Rs 99-499 per property.
- Premium listing: Rs 499-1999 per month.
- Lead charges for qualified tenant inquiries.
- Bronze, Silver, and Gold landlord subscriptions.
